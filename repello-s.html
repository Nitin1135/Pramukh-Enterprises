Repello-S

Products Details

Its ready to use, water based bonding enhancer strength between concrete and rebar’s and anti-corrosion compound.

It is developed by our R&D facility on the principles of modern age 21th century nanotechnology, it acts at molecular level of the steel surface and completely restores it to rust free condition due formation of dense interwoven nano-BSE formula on the surface of the rebar and penetration of liquid formulation in the fine pore & cracks of steel surface.

All type of re-bars used in RB & RCC reinforcement for enhancing bonding strength between concrete & bars and anti-corrosion protection

NANO BOND+™ is inorganic in nature with balanced pH for faster reaction with Fe ions and shortest process time with rebar’s.

  • Ready To Use at site pH Balanced Liquid*
  • Enhances Bond Strength Between Concrete and Reinforcement Bar’s up-to 100%*
  • Stops Internal Corrosion Process In Reinforced Bar’s*
  • On Site Application

Advantages And Characteristics

It enhances bond strength between rebar and concrete due to formation of nano-BSEF promotes bonding between rebar and concrete by upto 100%* compared to conventional method like uncoated, CPCC, epoxy, cement slurry coated rebar in reinforcement.

It penetrates into fine cracks & pore of rebar surface and forms a dense interwoven nano-BSEF which acts as barrier between rebar surface and atmosphere.

Interwoven nano-BSEF takes place all over rebar surface which helps to form coat within the surface and not over the surface.

The NANOBOND+ nano-BSEF creates its own space between the molecular gap of iron molecules and stops conversion process of Fe2 and Fe3 ions into hydroxyl carbonation i.e. Rust/Corrosion.

• Nano-BSEF arrangement is formed so densely that it doesn’t not allow gases and water to penetrate inside, thus it leads into stoppage from corroding.

How To Use

Surface Preparation:

No Specific Surface preparation required for fresh bars. (Clean only if any heavy dust or any other foreign impurities is attached to rebar’s. is to be removed)

Scrap out completely existing rust and cement slurry and other foreign matter from reinforced rebar in RCC (make the steel surface bare open)

NANO BOND+ Application:

Apply single coat of NANO BOND+ on rebar surface. By dip, spray or brush method only. Use only plastic material container for storage & time of application.

Allow the surface to get naturally dry (force drying if required is possible) drying time 30*min and curing time for 6-8*hrs and then can be used in reinforcement.

Dipping Time: Fresh steel 03 – 05* Sec Rusted steel 02 -03* Min

Storage And Recommendations

Keep material in dry place (Between 5˚C to 50˚C) (± 05˚C)

Store material in same material like as its original packing “HDPE or PVC” for storage as well as at the time of use (IT STAYS NEUTRAL WITH PLASTIC)

Always work in a well-ventilated space. Remove all contaminated and foreign material from the rebar surface to be treated before starting NANO BOND+ application

For best results, NANO BOND+ coating is to be used when the temperature of the rebar substrate is between 25oC & 55oC

Do not apply during rain

Do not dilute or mix any other product or solvents into NANO BOND+ liquid.

Keep coated rebar’s for drying on elevated platform to avoid direct contact with soil and dirt on site. Cover the coated bars after drying with plastic to avoid foreign matter settlement on it.

Clean your application equipment’s with clean normal water and store them in dry area.
